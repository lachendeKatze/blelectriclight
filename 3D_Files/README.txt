# Summary

I was inspired by a trip to Greenfield Village in Dearborn MI to produce an Edison style light bulb. Then I remix the Bulb Light by Even to make an LED light with single jumbo LED and 9v battery. Not real bright but makes a nice night light. Fun project to do with a parent and child to help teach about the history of the light bulb.
All the details are in my YouTube video:

https://youtu.be/cdclQpFlltc
